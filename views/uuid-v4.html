    What is a version 4 UUID?

    A Version 4 UUID (Universally Unique Identifier) is generated entirely using random numbers. It does not rely on timestamps or hardware information, making it ideal for scenarios where privacy and simplicity are important. The Version 4 UUIDs generated by this site use a cryptographically secure random number generator to ensure maximum uniqueness and safety.
